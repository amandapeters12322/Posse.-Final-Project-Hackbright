Plan

Whether it's a birthday party, a wedding, a group trip, or a small dinner, you'll find that planning your event is quick and easy with Posse. Create an account, create a new event, or check in on your events you're hosting or attending. It's that simple.

Invite

Once your event is created, search for friends who have created accounts to add them to your guest list. You'll see their RSVP once they respond. Connect with guests on Posse without blending social media friendships. Keep it professional or personal, it's your choice.

Stay Connected

Forget busy group text threads. No longer worry about event update notifications being lost in other notification threads. Post your comments, event updates, questions and more directly on the event page. Hosts and attending guests will receive instant SMS notifications when new posts are made.
